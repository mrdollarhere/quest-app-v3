# Frequently Asked Questions

### Can students use it without an account?
Yes! By default, "Guest Access" is enabled. Students just enter their name to start. You can turn this off in Settings if you want them to log in with an email.

### How many students can use it at once?
Because it uses Google Sheets, it can comfortably handle about 50-100 students at the same time. If you have thousands, you might hit Google's daily limits.

### Is the data secure?
Your data lives in YOUR Google account. We do not have access to it. The "Daily Access Key" helps prevent random people from finding your tests.

### Can I change the language?
Yes. The platform supports English, Vietnamese, and Spanish. Students can switch languages using the toggle at the top of the page.

### Can I export the results?
Yes. Go to any Student's profile in the Admin panel and click "Download Audit (CSV)" to get their scores in Excel format.

### What happens if Google Sheets is slow?
Google Sheets usually responds in 1-2 seconds. If it takes longer, the app will show a loading spinner. If it's completely offline, check your "Web App" deployment in Google.
